About me

Who am I? Thanks for asking!

I’m a designer who loves being creative in spaces where design truly matters to others. A journey shouldn’t take miles when a desire path already exists.

This is what I’m working on in my journey to becoming a UX designer. With my passion for problem-solving and my love for learning new techniques and skills, I strive to create seamless experiences that guide users effortlessly.

When I’m offline I spend my time creating art and enjoying the beauty of Wermlands nature.

My design philosophy

I believe that design should always meet the needs of users while staying true to the project itself. With the right amount of research and a willingness to iterate, a strong end result will always show through. One that speak to the users - the people.

Colors and shape languages speak more than text and therefor I tend to work with design that is understandable without neededing long texts to explain. Be that through a good hierarky and flow or just by having the right pictures and icons.

My UX journey

I have always loved being creative—whether through art, games, or storytelling. So when I first started my journey in an indie game course, I fell in love with the visual storytelling that UI and game art created. From there I realized that I could guide players and help them understand what to do without relying on words.

This path eventually led me to the world of UX, and that’s when everything clicked.

Now, I’m studying to become a full-fledged UX designer, with my passion for UI and visual storytelling guiding me through each project.
